How to speak Mandarin

Hanyu Pinyin

    Hanyu Pinyin is the Romanization (Latin alphabet like ours) of the Chinese characters based on their pronunciation. In other words, is a western-way to read Chinese characters. Is also, a system that allows you to write the characters "sound" in a digital or physical input like a keyboard and receive the character as a result on the screen. This makes the process for us, foreigners, to learn the language easier and for the whole world, to write them in modern devices
    Each character is formed by an initial (which generally is a consonant) and a final.

Tones

    Mandarin is a tonal language. This means that the cadence and the pitch of your voice will alter its meaning. The Chinese language counts of 4 tones that are different and concise throughout most of the spoken Mandarin world.

    Mastering this new concept is vital to start your journey into the Mandarin language. Good hearing and imitation skills are required.
